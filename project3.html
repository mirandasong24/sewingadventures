WATERFALL BLUE SKIRT

What did this piece used to look like?

A 100% polyester blue tablecloth; a circle with a 30 inch radius.

How did I go about upcycling this piece?

I saw this old tablecloth and immediately fell in love with the royal blue color. I measured the length and width of my body around my waist to approximate an oval, and I added a five-inch radius around those measurements and cut that larger oval from the tablecloth in an off-center position. I hemmed the waistline of the skirt and subsequently hand-sewed a button on the waistline to later fit the skirt to my waist. And there you have it - a classy royal blue skirt!
