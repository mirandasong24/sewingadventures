What did this piece used to look like?

This piece was originally a one-piece dress in size 14 for girls.

How did I go about upcycling this piece?

I cut the skirt off the dress and saved the half with the shirt, and I cropped it with a one-eighth-inch stitch all around the bottom. I styled it to become an off-the-shoulder top, but the original dress was to be worn on the shoulders.
